Who We Are

The Potomac Glen School Boundary Task Force is a volunteer-led group working to represent and protect the interests of our community during the MCPS boundary review process. The goal of this task force is to unify the community, ensuring that all voices are heard and no member feels unfairly impacted.

What Is Happening

  • MCPS plans to open Crown High School for the 2027–2028 school year.
  • MCPS has proposed four redistricting options, all of which would reassign Wayside Elementary School away from Churchill High School.
  • Two options split Lakewood ES. Neighborhoods like Hunting woods, Potomac country corner and others were negatively impacted.
  • These changes risk splitting our neighborhood and causing significant social disruption for students and families.
